Temperature vs. Latitude




The scatter plot shown here illustrates temperature vs. latitude. To no one's surprise (hopefully), temperature increases as one approaches the equator. Depending on the time of year, we would also expect to see a disparity in temperatures between the northern and southern hemispheres (positive and negave latitudes respectively). As indicated, the temperature data was pulled in the month of June, so we do not see such a disparity between the two hemispheres.
